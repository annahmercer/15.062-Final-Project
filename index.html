Background

Here at Game Show Contestant (GSC) Consulting, we specialize in preparing future game show participants for their appearances by utilizing key metrics to perform historical analyses and predictive modeling. These data-based processes provide participants with everything they need to know to maximize their chances of winning the game show of their choice.

Each quarter, we expand our services to accommodate future contestants from different game shows. This quarter, we are happy to announce that we are specifically looking to partner with future contestants of Food Network's Chopped™.

Ingredient Trends


By viewing the bar charts to the left, we see that there is not a significant difference in the types of ingredients that appear in the baskets during different seasons of the year.

Through looking at the dataset, it appears that there is a relationship between the proximity of an episode's air date to a holiday and the basket ingredients in an episode. For example, a number of episodes that were specified as Thanksgiving themed or that aired around Thanksgiving included basket ingredients connected to Thanksgiving such as a whole turkey and pumpkin pie flavored ice cream.
